- **High quality of life**

- **Remote work visas and friendly policies**

## Top nomad destinations in 2025

### 1️⃣ Lisbon, Portugal

Lisbon continues to be a favorite due to its vibrant culture, mild climate, and growing tech scene.

**Why Lisbon?**

- **Fast Wi-Fi**, with an average speed of **120 Mbps**, ensuring smooth workflows.
- Affordable **co-living spaces** that foster **community building** among **nomads**.
- A lower cost of living compared to other Western European capitals.
- Expanding remote work incentives, including **visa options** for digital nomads.
- A thriving cafe culture, perfect for working outside traditional office spaces.
- A rich history, stunning architecture, and breathtaking viewpoints.
- Access to **beaches and nature** just a short train ride away, allowing for an excellent work-life balance.

### 2️⃣ Chiang Mai, Thailand

A longtime haven for digital nomads.

**Why Chiang Mai?**

- **Ultra-affordable living costs**, with monthly expenses as low as **$800**.
- Coworking hubs with **gigabit-speed internet**, such as Punspace and Yellow.
- A welcoming community of **remote workers** and entrepreneurs.
- A rich cultural experience with temples, night markets, and a thriving cafe scene.
- Numerous workshops, networking events, and entrepreneurial meetups.
- Access to scenic mountains, national parks, and outdoor adventures.
- A relaxed pace of life, with a focus on well-being, meditation, and fitness.


---

### 3️⃣ Medellín, Colombia

Now a leading destination in Latin America.

**Why Medellín?**

- **Fiber-optic internet** with speeds up to **300 Mbps** in key districts.
- A rapidly growing expat and nomad community.
- Affordable **co-living and coworking spaces** designed for productivity.
- Year-round **spring-like weather** and abundant green spaces.
- Easy access to outdoor adventures, including hiking, paragliding, and weekend getaways to coffee plantations.
- An efficient metro system, making it one of the best-connected cities in South America.
- A dynamic cultural scene, including art galleries, music festivals, and a strong focus on innovation.


---

### 4️⃣ Mexico City, Mexico

Perfect for big city lovers.

**Why Mexico City?**

- **Fast and stable Wi-Fi** (150 Mbps+ in coworking spaces).
- A dynamic cultural scene, world-class cuisine, and historical richness.
- Affordable and stylish **co-living** options.
- A growing ecosystem of remote workers and international entrepreneurs.
- A variety of neighborhoods, from vibrant urban hubs like Condesa and Roma to peaceful residential areas.
- One of the best street food scenes in the world, alongside Michelin-starred restaurants.
- A well-connected transport system, including metro, buses, and bike lanes.
- Weekend getaways to destinations like Teotihuacán, Valle de Bravo, and Tepoztlán.


---

### 5️⃣ Bali, Indonesia

A paradise for digital nomads.

**Why Bali?**

- Coworking spaces like **Dojo and Outpost**, offering **high-speed Wi-Fi** and community events.
- An unparalleled work-life balance with **beaches, yoga, and eco-friendly living**.
- A **low cost of living** compared to Western destinations.
- Emerging sustainability initiatives making it an attractive **eco-conscious hub**.
- A deep-rooted wellness culture with retreats, healthy cafes, and mindfulness spaces.
- A wide variety of landscapes, from jungle retreats to beachfront coworking spots.
- A friendly and welcoming local culture, with a deep sense of spirituality and hospitality.
